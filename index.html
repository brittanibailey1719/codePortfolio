About Me

Brittani is a recent graduate from Hampton University in Hampton Virginia with a Bachelor's Degree in Strategic Communications and Environmental Science. Her interest in journalism and science came together through her opportunities interning at the Dekalb County Board of Health, The Center for Climate and Energy Solutions and even the National Institute of Aerospace in the Communications department for each location. After working two years in Technical Recruiting for major companies like ADP, Bank of America and Blue Cross Blue Shield, She gained an interest in becoming a developer. She is currently enrolled in the Georgia Tech Bootcamp aspiring to be a Software Engineer in the near future.
